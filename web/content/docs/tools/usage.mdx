## Returns

A `UsageResponse` with:

- `total_budget`, `estimated_used`, `estimated_remaining` — token accounting against the configured window.
- `level` — `normal`, `elevated`, or `critical`.
- `drop_candidates` — content ministr suggests dropping.
- `prefetch_metrics` and `session_metrics` — the latter includes `total_deliveries`, `cumulative_tokens_delivered`, `total_evictions`, `cumulative_tokens_evicted`, `total_compressions`, `cumulative_tokens_compressed`, `total_tokens_saved`, `compression_ratio`, `delta_updates`, and `dedup_hits`.
- `schema_tokens`, `tool_count`, `coherence_alerts`, `elicitation_evicted`.

## Notes

- Under non-`normal` pressure, ministr may elicit drop choices from the client and evict them; `elicitation_evicted` reflects this.
- The numbers track what ministr delivered, not what your agent actually retains — treat them as a hint, never a stop signal.
